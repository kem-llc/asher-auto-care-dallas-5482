Our story

Family-Owned Auto Care in Dallas

To provide honest, high-quality auto repair services that keep our customers safe and satisfied.

Asher Auto Care was founded with a simple mission: treat every customer like family. Our owner, a lifelong Dallas resident and ASE-certified master technician, saw a need for transparent, trustworthy auto repair in the community. What started as a small two-bay shop has grown into a full-service facility serving thousands of satisfied customers.

We believe in doing the job right the first time. Our team stays up-to-date with the latest automotive technology and uses premium parts to ensure lasting repairs. Whether it's a simple oil change or a complex engine overhaul, we treat every vehicle with the same care and attention we'd give our own.
