Our story

Dallas's Trusted Auto Care Team

To provide honest, high-quality auto repair that keeps Dallas drivers safe and satisfied.

Asher Auto Care was founded with a simple mission: bring integrity back to auto repair. Our founder, a master technician with over 15 years of experience, saw too many customers being overcharged for unnecessary work. He built a shop where transparency and quality come first. Today, we serve the Dallas community with the same values: honesty, expertise, and a commitment to doing the job right the first time.

Our team of ASE-certified technicians uses the latest diagnostic tools and high-quality parts to service all makes and models. We treat every customer like a neighbor, explaining repairs in plain language and offering fair, upfront pricing. Whether you need a quick oil change or a major engine repair, we treat your car with the same care we would our own.
