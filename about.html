Our story

Your Trusted Auto Repair Partner in Dallas

Our mission is to provide honest, high-quality auto repair services that keep our community safe and mobile. We believe in transparency, education, and building long-term relationships with our customers.

Asher Auto Care was founded on the belief that car repairs shouldn't be a hassle. With over 15 years of experience in the automotive industry, our founder saw the need for a shop that prioritizes customer trust and technical excellence. We opened our doors in Dallas with a simple promise: diagnose accurately, fix right, and treat every customer like family.

Today, we've served hundreds of vehicles in the Dallas area, earning a 4.9-star rating through our commitment to quality. Our team continues to invest in training and equipment to stay ahead of automotive technology. We're proud to be a local business that gives back to the community we serve.
